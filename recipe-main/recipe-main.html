Simple Omelete Recipe

Any and quick for any meal. This classic omelete combines beaten beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally fillerd with you choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

preparation time

  • total:approximately 10 minutes
  • preparation:5 minutes
  • cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional fillings : cheese, diced vegetable, cooked meats, herbs

Instuctions

  1. Beat the eggs: in a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add aa tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffer texture.

  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium het and add butter or oil

  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.

  4. Add fillings (opsional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slighty runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the image-omelette

  5. Fold and serve: As the omeltte continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide in onto a plate.

  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
